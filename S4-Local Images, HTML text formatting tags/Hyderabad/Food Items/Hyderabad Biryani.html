Hyderabad Biryani

Hyderabadi Biryani is said to be more spicy and savoury. It is made in the form of Dum and has a lot of Zaffron. Combining basmati rice and chicken makes Hyderabadi biryani distinguishable from others. The presentation and the pungent spices are the hallmarks of Hyderabadi
